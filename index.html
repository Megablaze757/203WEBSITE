About Us

Traditional craft, contemporary finish

At 203 Carpentry, we blend time-honoured joinery techniques with modern design sensibility. Every project starts with a conversation — understanding your space, your taste, and how you live in it.

Based in Sussex, we work across the county delivering bespoke built-in cabinetry, media walls, bathroom panelling, period windows and fitted bedroom furniture. Each piece is made to measure and finished by hand.
